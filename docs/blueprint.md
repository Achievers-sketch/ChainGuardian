# **App Name**: ChainGuardian

## Core Features:

- Manufacturer Product Registration: Manufacturers can register their products by providing product details, certifications, and compliance documents.
- QR Code Generation: The platform automatically generates a unique, encrypted QR code for each product, linking to its verification page.
- Consumer Verification: Consumers can scan the QR code to instantly verify the authenticity of the product and view its complete supply chain journey.
- Cold Chain Monitoring: IoT sensors collect and log temperature data throughout the supply chain, ensuring products are maintained within required temperature ranges. Thresholds are compared to ranges established as guidelines for industries in which 'cold chain' style protection is required.
- Real-Time Alerts: The system sends real-time alerts via SMS/Email/Push notifications when temperature breaches or other anomalies occur.
- Counterfeit Detection: AI tool to analyze scan patterns and geographical data to identify potential counterfeit products.
- USSD Code Verification: Enables verification via USSD codes for feature phone users in areas with limited internet access.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep blue (#2E4057) to convey trust and security in supply chain integrity.
- Background color: Light gray (#F0F4F8), almost white, to ensure legibility and a clean, modern interface.
- Accent color: Teal (#26A69A) to highlight important actions and alerts, indicating reliable tracking and verification processes.
- Body font: 'PT Sans', a versatile and readable humanist sans-serif for body text.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk', for a computerized look befitting a product focused on technology and blockchain. 
- Use clear and intuitive icons for each checkpoint in the supply chain and different product categories.
- Mobile-first responsive design with a focus on readability and ease of use, especially for low-tech users.
